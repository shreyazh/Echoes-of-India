Acknowledgments

This project would not have been possible without the dedicated work of archaeologists, historians, and scholars who have preserved India's rich heritage through meticulous research and documentation.

Special recognition goes to the Archaeological Survey of India, the National Archives, and numerous universities and research institutions that have made their collections accessible to the public.

We are particularly grateful to the communities and families who have preserved oral traditions and personal narratives that bring human voices to historical events.

This educational resource is dedicated to all those whose stories have been marginalized or forgotten in traditional historical narratives.
